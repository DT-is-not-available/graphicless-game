FIRST RELEASE: Alpha 0.1
New features:
  +  player (WASD and SPACE to control)
  +  solid ground (very solid)
  +  semisolids (jump through)
  +  moving platforms (they move)
  +  falling platforms (they fall when you touch them)
  +  crumble blocks (can be destroyed by hitting from below or "trailing" into them)
  +  powerups:
       +  double jump (jump once while in mid-air)
	   +  dash (SPACE+A or SPACE+D to dash in a direction while in mid-air, can break crumble blocks)
